Why Telemetry?

I develop server-side modifications for DayZ. Since they are not loaded by clients and can be obtained via various methods (Steam Workshop, GitHub, or built from source), I have zero visibility on their actual usage.

Metrics = Motivation
Knowing that my work brings value is my fuel. Disabling telemetry disables my motivation.

Privacy First

  • No personal data collected.
  • Only App Name, Version, Type and Port.
  • One request per restart.
